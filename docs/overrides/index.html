Lighter

Framework for streamlining and simplifying
deep learning experiments

Say goodbye to messy scripts and forgetting parameters.

Discover how our ML framework can revamp your research code and experience. Lighter is here to help you organize your experiments and make sense of what you ran (even years ago).

Join us in our reproducibility and transparency journey!!

Features

Structured

Our structure ensures that your experiments are standardized, organized, and reproducible.

Boilerplate-free

Forget about writing training logic over and over.
From classification to self-supervised learning.

Readable

Wondering what learning rate or architecture you used? Just glance at the config.

Modular

Override parameters easily.
Leave argparse and hardcoding in the past.
